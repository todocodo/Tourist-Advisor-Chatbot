Tourist Advisor Chatbot

This chatbot can hold a basic conversation with a person and recommend restaurants and pubs, and also give information about the weather. It does work with a database, so the chatbot is not able to remember what you said previously.

The chatbot recognises the following intents:
  • Greetings
  • Goodbye
  • Thank you
  • Bar suggestion
  • Restaurant suggestion
  • Weather
  • Out of Scope
If you ask for something that is not part of that list, the bot will ask for clarification.
